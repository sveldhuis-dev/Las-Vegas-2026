Wynn / Encore Restaurants (On Property)
Supper Club · Entertainment

Delilah

Golden Age Hollywood supper club. Live entertainment, classic cocktails, showgirl atmosphere. Perfect for a big group Saturday night — theatrical, fun, memorable. Book early.

→ Beef Wellington, tableside Caesar, signature cocktails
Steakhouse · Lakefront

SW Steakhouse

Wynn's signature steakhouse with a lakefront terrace — water views, exceptional steaks, great for group dinners. Thursday night arrival dinner is ideal here.

→ Wagyu strip, lobster bisque, tableside smores
Japanese · Fine Dining

Mizumi

Forbes Five-Star Japanese restaurant. Chef Min Kim's artful kaiseki, sushi, teppan, tempura. One of the finest Japanese restaurants in the US. Reserve well in advance.

→ Omakase sushi, wagyu teppan, Japanese A5 beef
Mexican Coastal

Casa Playa

Vibrant coastal Mexican at Wynn. Elevated tacos, ceviche, mezcal cocktails. More casual and lively than SW or Mizumi — good group energy for Friday night.

→ Wagyu birria tacos, whole fried fish, mezcal cocktails
Cocktail Bar · Bites

Overlook Lounge

Above the Lake of Dreams. Fashion and film-inspired cocktails, small plates. Perfect pre-dinner drinks or late-night gathering spot. No reservation needed — first come first served.

→ The Bond (Negroni variation), oysters, charcuterie

Wynn Revelry by Chef's Table

Once-a-year event during your exact stay — 25+ Michelin-starred chefs, 71 James Beard Award honors. Icons Dinner (Fri), Chef Cookoff (Sat), Golf Tournament (Sat), Grand Tasting (Sun).

→ lasvegasrevelry.com for tickets — buy now
Off-Property Strip Picks

Carbone Las Vegas
